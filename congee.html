Congee Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of rice
  • 2 cups of chicken broth
  • 6 cups of water
  • sliced ginger
  • pork floss (optional)
  • chili oil
  • salt
  • pepper
  • a stalk and sliced green onions

Recipe

  1. Wash the rice (only once to get rid of the impurities).
  2. Add it to a pot with chicken broth and water.
  3. Add ginger and a stalk of green onions.
  4. Let it simmer in medium heat.
  5. Once it thickens and the grains are mushy, turn the heat off.
  6. Season with salt, pepper, and chili oil.
  7. Garnish with green onions.
  8. Add pork floss (optional).
